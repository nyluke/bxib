Who We Are

The Bronx has more parkland than any other borough

The Bronx contains 24% parkland—more than any other borough—but much of it is located on the periphery, leaving smaller neighborhood parks under-resourced and understaffed. We focus on these local green spaces, making nature accessible to every community.

  • 🌱
    A mature tree absorbs 48+ pounds of carbon monoxide per year—critical in the borough with the state's highest asthma rates
  • 🌎
    Weekend events, school partnerships, and corporate team-building that bring communities together
  • 🎓
    Youth-led programming that develops real leadership skills and creates pathways to green careers

What We Do

Our Programs

From community gardening to youth leadership, our programs create lasting change across the Bronx.

🌳

GREEN Initiative

Our signature community program—Grow, Revitalize, Engage, Educate, Nurture. Volunteers learn to revitalize green spaces while understanding their environmental impact.

🌟

PLACE Youth Program

Since 2013, Bronx youth lead groups of 15–150 volunteers in environmental service projects, gaining horticultural training and real work experience.

🌲

Tree Giveaways

Free trees for Bronx residents to increase canopy cover, improve air quality, and beautify neighborhoods—one tree at a time.

👥

Community Events

Regular volunteer days at parks across the Bronx—from Soundview to Crotona to St. Mary's. Weekday and weekend opportunities for everyone.

🎓

Education & Outreach

School partnerships, guest speakers from environmental organizations, and college tours—broadening horizons for Bronx youth.

🤝

Corporate Partnerships

Team-building service days for companies that want to give back. A hands-on way to make a visible impact in the community.
